# Spotify-Data-Analysis
provides a comprehensive analysis of music trends, artist performance, and the technical components that drive track popularity.
The "2025 Spotify Review: Music Insights" report, authored by Vedansh Shirvastava, provides a comprehensive analysis of music trends, artist performance, and the technical components that drive track popularity.

### Key Performance Highlights

* **Release Growth**: Music releases have grown significantly, reaching approximately **2,000 releases** by 2025.


* **Artist Statistics**: The platform features **3,996 artists** and a total of **4,351 released tracks**.


* **Top Performer**: **Lady Gaga** is identified as the most popular artist.


* **Leading Track & Album**: both the most popular track and album are titled "**Die With A Smile**".


* **Most Active Artist**: **Bad Bunny** is the most prolific artist, with over **80 releases**.



### Genre and Sub-genre Insights

* **Diversity**: The report tracks **35 distinct genres** and **84 sub-genres**.


* **Most Played Genre**: **Electronic** music is the most played genre.


* **Most Played Sub-genre**: **Modern** is the top-performing sub-genre.



### Music Component Analysis

The report categorizes popularity into four tiers: Not Good (0-25%), Not Bad (25-50%), Average (50-75%), and Popular (75%+). To achieve "Popular" status, the analysis suggests a specific technical profile for songs:

| Component | Target Range/Level |
| --- | --- |
| **Energy & Loudness** | High |
| **Danceability** | Higher side |
| **Tempo** | Between 100 - 175 BPM |
| **Keynotes** | Around 1 to 5  |
| **Instrumentalness & Acousticness** | Low  |
| **Liveness & Speechiness** | Low  |
| **Valence** | Anything except Low  |
